The Human Liver

A brief overview of one of the body's most important organs.

What is the Liver?

The liver is a large, reddish-brown organ located in the upper right side of the abdomen. It plays a crucial role in many bodily functions.

Functions of the Liver

  • Detoxifies harmful substances
  • Produces bile for digestion
  • Stores glycogen (a form of sugar)
  • Helps in metabolism of fats and proteins
  • Processes nutrients from food
